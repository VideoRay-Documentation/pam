Generic Protocol Adapter Multiplixer (PAM) Overview

VideoRay is pleased to introduce the PAM accessory module for your PRO 4 ROV. The PAM module allows for the connection of a low bandwith serial device to your PRO 4 via the 9-pin accessory port, adding extreme customization and versatility in accessories. Along with our Cockpit software, the PAM accessory acts as a transperant accessory for your connected serial device. The PAM supports both 12v and 24V pass through connections.
