Requirements

The PAM is intended for users who have familiarity with their custom device and would like to integrate it with a VideoRay Pro 4. The PAM will require soldering power and data communications lines. Programming the PAM is a simple process, but understanding the information coming from your device requires familiarity with its data protocol. After soldered and programmed, the PAM needs to be enclosed in a waterproof housing.

In most cases, how to operate your device during a dive will be included in the device's operating manual. Instructions for how to operate all devices compatible with the PAM during a dive are outside the scope of this document, but we encourage you to review any documents pertaining to your device to ensure the proper usage of all equipment.
