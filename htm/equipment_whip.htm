9-Pin Whip

The 9-Pin whip included with your VideoRay splits the 9-pin accessory port cable into individual wires, allowing them to be soldered to the PAM board. Every pin is numbered on the front and back of the accessory port connector, and terminates in an individual wire at the opposite end of the whip. Each carries a different type of data to or from the ROV and is color coded, as follows:

Pin Function Color
1 Video - Black
2 Video + White
3 24V DC + (30 Watts) Red
4 Aux + (APIC) Connects to tether pin 4 and control panel aux port pin 7 Green
5 Power Common (Ground) Orange
6 Aux - (APIC) Connects to tether pin 5 and control panel aux port pin 8 Yellow
7 RS-485 -/A Blue
8 RS-485 +/B Grey
9 12V DC + (30 Watts) Brown
